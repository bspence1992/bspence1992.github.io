The "An Ember in the Ashes" series is a four-book young adult fantasy quartet written by Sabaa Tahir, featuring characters Laia and Elias in a militaristic world inspired by ancient Rome.
The story follows their intertwined destinies as they navigate a society where a brutal military empire oppresses scholars like Laia, forcing her to become a spy to save her family, while Elias, a soldier, seeks freedom.

Books included in the series:

  1. An Ember in the Ashes
  2. A Torch Against the Night
  3. A Reaper at the Gates
  4. Sky Beyond the Storm
